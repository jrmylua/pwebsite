Music has always been one of the most important parts of my life. Early in my childhood, I learned how to play the piano, which built the musical foundation that lead to learning to play the violin and guitar. Throughout elementary school, middle school, and high school, I actively participated in the school orchestras, however in college I decided to try something new - acapella.
I always enjoyed singing as a child, but never took singing seriously. One day during senior year of high school, however, I decided to make a cover with one of my friends, and received surprising amounts of positive feedback from it. Ever since, I started creating more song covers which are all displayed on this page for your enjoyment.
